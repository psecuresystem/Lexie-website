Online calendar made social

  • It eliminates the additional step of integrating to an external calendar prior to saving calendar events.

Lectie features

Customizale calendar dashboard

View everything from your calendar dashboard. Filter and search your calendars whichever way you want, and view events by RSVP status, event name, or event organizer. Put all your events in one place.

Simple & Effective Item Creation

Create meetings, tasks, events, and status updates. Update these items in real time with the powerful cloud technology.

Privacy Settings

Manage the privacy of your calendar updates with personal, private, and public privacy settings. Share data across secure cloud connections so that only authorized eyes can view your updates.

Unique URL and QR Code Access

Generate unique URLs and QR codes for profile pages within Lectie. Do the same for individual events and share them to enable others connect with you

Full Visibility Across the Whole Platform

Use the Explore feature to search for all listed public events. Filter events by category and location, or share public events of your own so that other users can sign up.

Integration with Social Platforms

Share your events across different social media platforms with the tap of a button. View other attendees and share your planned attendance.

Event detail page and Agenda Tools

Craft event agendas for group meetings, academic conferences, union discussions, trade exhibitions, and other events, then make these accessible on your event detail page. Display detailed event information within the dedicated event page and update this information in real time so that all users are working with the latest information.

Cloud hosting and Security

Lectie is a cloud-hosted secure platform. Data sharing and transfer are underpinned by industry-leading security protocols that ensure complete safety and compliance.

Get Notifications and Reminders to your mobile device

Set specific notifications as you create your events, tasks, or meetings. Manage notification and reminder settings within the platform.
